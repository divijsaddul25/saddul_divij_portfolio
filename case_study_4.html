Ile Cafe Brand Identity

le Café is an exciting new Mauritian-inspired café brand that is currently under construction, with locations planned in both London, Ontario and Oshawa, Canada. This vibrant brand is set to offer a unique cultural experience, blending the flavors and atmosphere of Mauritius with the local community. I had the privilege of working on the brand’s identity, including designing the logo, creating a logo animation, and developing web design and mockups. While the café is still in the works, the vision for Ile Café is coming to life, and it promises to be an exciting addition to the Canadian café scene, with its launch just around the corner.
