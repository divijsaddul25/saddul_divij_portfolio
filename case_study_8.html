Audia Earbud

For this project, I created a detailed 3D model of earbud designs using Cinema 4D (C4D), bringing the product to life with realistic textures and lighting. I then used the rendered images to create striking posters that showcased the earbuds from various angles, emphasizing both their sleek design and advanced features. To enhance the user experience, I also developed a model viewer for the website, allowing visitors to interact with the earbuds in 3D and explore them in detail. Additionally, I produced a commercial that highlights the key selling points of the product, demonstrating its functionality and style. The project also featured an X-ray view, offering a unique look inside the earbuds to highlight their engineering and innovative technology. The full website was designed to seamlessly integrate all these elements, providing an immersive and informative experience for users.
