Kavorka Rebrand

For this team assignment, I worked with my partner to develop a comprehensive brand identity for Kavorka, a cosmetic brand specializing in body wash and moisturizers. I focused on creating the brand's visual identity, including the logo and overall design elements, while my partner worked on crafting the mockups for the products. Together, we designed Instagram and Facebook banner ads to promote the brand. Additionally, we collaborated on producing a logo animation and a commercial to further enhance the brand's promotional efforts and engage the audience.

Problem:

As we developed the brand identity for Kavorka, we faced a significant challenge: how to create a logo that was both unique and memorable, yet still conveyed the essence of a luxurious, high-quality cosmetic brand. We wanted the logo to stand out in a crowded beauty industry while ensuring it felt approachable and reflective of the body wash and moisturizer products Kavorka offers.

Solution:

To address this, we focused on crafting a logo that combined elegance with simplicity. We drew inspiration from nature, using soft, organic shapes that represented the brand's emphasis on gentle, nourishing ingredients. The use of modern typography, paired with a subtle color palette, helped create a sophisticated yet inviting look.
