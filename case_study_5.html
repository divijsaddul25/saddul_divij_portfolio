Reebok Marketing Campaign

For our team-based assignment, we developed a comprehensive marketing campaign for Reebok's EasyTone shoes targeted at men in their 30s who work a 9-to-5 job. We utilized a blend of AI-generated imagery and real photographs to create visually striking posters. These images were then carefully edited and enhanced using Photoshop and Illustrator to ensure they aligned with the brand's aesthetic and effectively captured the attention of our target audience. The campaign aimed to showcase the shoes' unique benefits, combining style and comfort for men leading active, professional lifestyles. The final product reflects a modern, dynamic approach, strategically designed to resonate with our demographic.
